What is it?

Is this a Poshmark bot? ...Poshibly. If you're an active seller on Poshmark, we can help you with automated engagement and take out the leg work of sharing your closet. Poshibly is a Chrome extension that will help you do a couple basic things. Check it out below. And yes, it's a bot.

What Can It Do?

Poshibly is an automated share tool that saves hours of engagement for Poshmark resellers. With Poshibly, you'll be able to share:
  • to your closet, or someone else's
  • to followers or to the current party
  • infinitely
  • on an interval
  • in reverse order (non-reverse by default)
  • randomly
  • a specific amount of items
  • items to party based on filter keywords
