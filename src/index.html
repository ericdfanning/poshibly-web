Is this a bot?...poshibly.
If you're an active seller on Poshmark, we can help you with engagement and take out the leg work of sharing your closest. Poshibly is a Chrome extension that will help you share your closet to followers and parties, follow back and share items for other Poshers who shared yours. And yes, it's a bot.
