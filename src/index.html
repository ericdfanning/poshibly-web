What is it?

Is this a Poshmark bot? ...Poshibly. If you're an active seller on Poshmark, we can help you with automated engagement and take out the leg work of sharing your closet. This tool can save you hours a day of manual engagement! Poshibly is a Chrome extension that will help you do a couple basic things. Check it out below. And yes, it's a bot.

What Can It Do?

Poshibly is an automated share tool that saves hours of engagement for Poshmark resellers. With Poshibly, you'll be able to share your closet or someone else's:

  • to followers
  • infinitely
  • on an interval
  • in reverse order (non-reverse by default)
  • randomly
  • a specific amount of items
